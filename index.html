Jimbu Himalayan Restaurant offers an authentic taste of the Himalayas. Enjoy traditional dishes made from fresh, organic ingredients sourced locally. Our signature dish, Jimbu Soup, is a must-try for anyone craving a flavorful, hearty meal.

Thamel Restaurant is inspired by the vibrant street food scene in the heart of Kathmandu. We bring you flavors that are both bold and authentic, with dishes like our spiced curries, grilled meats, and traditional desserts that are sure to satisfy your cravings.
